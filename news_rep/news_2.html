RAID: Shadow Legends Patch 10.60 Adds Shadow Arena and Major QOL Improvements

Reddit July, 2025

Update 10.60 packs a ton of quality-of-life improvements: blind matchups in the Platinum Tier Classic Arena, a Super Raids Event in the Minotaur’s Labyrinth, minor improvements to the Clan Shop, Siege, Doom Tower, and more. We’ve covered the most important ones in the Update Highlights article below - take a look if you want to learn the details!

Classic Arena Shadow Phase

Over time, we received much feedback about powerful Clans teaming against other players in the Classic Arena Platinum Tier. While such cooperation is perfectly understandable, we want to ensure the Platinum Tier remains a fair and open challenge for everyone. To that end, Shadow Phase will be introduced in Update 10.60.

It will activate bi-weekly on Monday at 00:00 UTC and last for 8 hours before the Arena reset. All information about your opponents in the Platinum Tier will be concealed for the duration of the Shadow Phase - no usernames, no Clan tags to identify the owner of the Team you are about to fight. This way, staying in the Platinum Tier will rely on your personal Arena setup rather than on membership in specific Clans.

Minotaur’s Labyrinth Super Raids

We’ll launch a Super Raids Event in the Minotaur’s Labyrinth when Update 10.60 goes live. The feature will function in the same manner it does in other Dungeons: spend twice the Energy to earn double rewards in a single run.

Additionally, we’re adding a pop-up warning that will inform you if some Champions in your Minotaur Battle Team have maxed-out Mastery Scrolls. And if some Champions max out their Scrolls during Multi-Battles, the ongoing Multi-Battle sequence will be stopped.

As of Update 10.60, the game will save Team Presets from previous matching rotations and completed battles (whether you won or lost) for Doom Tower Bosses and Secret Rooms. All Skill priorities will remain unchanged.
